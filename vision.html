Why Mo

Toronto is too slow, too expensive, and too closed. Mo Khan is running to accelerate the city so people can actually build their lives here.

This campaign is about more than criticism. It is about making Toronto faster, more open, more affordable, and more opportunity-rich for students, workers, families, entrepreneurs, and small businesses.

What makes this campaign different

Speed

Mo’s campaign is built around acceleration — faster housing, faster permits, faster service delivery, and faster opportunities for people trying to move forward.

Opportunity

This is not a politics of decline. It is a politics of upward motion, local growth, and making Toronto a city people can still build a future in.

City-building

The campaign is focused on housing, talent retention, local business, nightlife, and modernizing City Hall so the city actually works better.

Meeting people where they are

The campaign grows through real conversations with students, business owners, faith communities, organizers, and neighborhoods across Toronto.

The core idea

Toronto should be fast, opportunity-rich, entrepreneurial, youth-retaining, and open for business. This campaign is about making that real.
