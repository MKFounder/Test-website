The platform

What Mo
will do

Concrete actions, not campaign promises. Six priorities Mo will move on from day one at City Hall.

01
Freeze the rent

Cap rent increases at inflation, close renoviction loopholes, and create a tenant protection office that actually picks up the phone.

02
Fix the TTC

Free transit for students and seniors. Faster service on the busiest routes. Real accountability for every delay and every cancelled bus.

03
Build housing fast

Cut red tape on missing middle housing, end exclusionary zoning near transit, and open 10,000 affordable units in the first term.

04
Safe communities

Invest in mental health crisis response, youth programming, and community safety — prevention over reaction, always.

05
Small biz first

Cut licensing friction, create a dedicated small business desk at City Hall, and fight for commercial rent relief for shops being priced out.

06
Open City Hall

Live-streamed council meetings. Multilingual services. A Mayor's office that shows up in Scarborough, Etobicoke, and every ward in between.
