The signature commitments

Toronto is too slow.
Mo will fix that.

Five concrete guarantees — with deadlines, accountability, and consequences if City Hall misses them. Not promises. Commitments.

30
Day guarantee
Housing permit turnaround

Complete, compliant applications get an approval or deficiency list within 30 days. No more year-long waits.

48
Hour guarantee
Small business permits

Signage, patios, minor renos, low-risk events — 48 hours or you get a fee rebate. Full stop.

20
% mandate
Local procurement

20% of eligible City contracts go to Toronto startups and SMEs. City Hall becomes a platform for local firms.

24
/7 city
Night economy reform

Designated zones with default extended hours. Toronto should be open for business and open for life.

AI
Powered
City Hall modernization

AI speeds up permits, routes complaints, fixes potholes faster. Government at the speed of people.

All policy areas

The full
agenda

Eight interconnected policy areas, one underlying belief: Toronto should be faster, more open, and work for everyone.

Housing
Build faster, live better

30-day permit guarantee for low-rise, multiplex, garden suites, and laneway suites. Pre-approved templates. Fee rebates for missed deadlines. Make it easy for homeowners to add floors, units, and suites.

Permit guaranteeHomeowner intensificationStudent housing fast-track
Local economy
Keep money in Toronto

20% of eligible City contracts go to local firms. Break large contracts into smaller pieces. A paid pilot pathway so local firms can prove their solutions and scale them citywide.

20% mandateContract unbundlingStartup pilot pathwayProcurement dashboard
Small business
Easiest city to run a business

48-hour permit guarantee for low-risk applications. Every new city bylaw gets a small business impact review. Make Toronto the easiest big city in North America to open, operate, and grow a business.

48-hour permitsImpact assessmentBylaw simplification
Night economy
Toronto After Dark

Designated nightlife zones with default extended hours. Simpler permits for clubs, bars, and music venues. Late-night transit reliability. A city that's actually alive.

Extended hours zonesLate-night transitArts & culture support
AI & civic tech
Government at the speed of people

AI speeds up permit processing, routes complaints, and fixes potholes faster. Ward-level civic innovation program. AI literacy for everyone — through libraries, colleges, and senior workshops.

AI permit processingWard innovation programPublic AI literacy
Youth & students
Build your life here

Toronto should be the best city to start your life. Student housing fast-tracks with rent caps. Targeted transit affordability. Civic fellowships. Talent retention.

Student housingTalent retentionCivic fellowshipsTransit affordability
City Hall reform
Leaner, faster, accountable

Cap administrative growth. Reduce consultant reliance. Digitize permits and licensing. Outcome-based program reviews. A public performance dashboard so Torontonians can hold City Hall accountable.

Admin capDigitizationPerformance dashboardOutcome reviews
Safety & public life
Safety plus opportunity

Investment in mental health crisis response, youth programming, and well-lit public spaces. Stagnation is not safety. A city that's alive is a city that's safe.

Crisis responseYouth programmingPublic space design
