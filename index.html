The platform

What Mo
will do

01
Freeze the rent

Cap rent increases at inflation, close renoviction loopholes, and create a tenant protection office that actually picks up the phone.

02
Fix the TTC

Free transit for students and seniors. Faster service on the busiest routes. Real accountability for every delay and every cancelled bus.

03
Build housing fast

Cut red tape on missing middle housing, end exclusionary zoning near transit, and open 10,000 affordable units in the first term.

04
Safe communities

Invest in mental health crisis response, youth programming, and community safety — prevention over reaction, always.

05
Small biz first

Cut licensing friction, create a dedicated City Hall small business desk, and fight for commercial rent relief for shops being priced out.

06
Open City Hall

Live-streamed council meetings. Multilingual services. A Mayor's office that meets people in Scarborough, Etobicoke, and every ward in between.

Why Mo

Built for
this city

Mo understands affordability, transit, safety, and accountability — because he's lived them. A campaign built with students, workers, new Canadians, and the communities City Hall too often ignores.

Students
Campus to city

Free transit, affordable housing near campuses, and a mayor who understands what it actually costs to start your life in Toronto right now.

New Canadians
New roots, new voice

Multilingual services, fair access to housing, and representation that reflects the city Toronto actually is — not the one it used to be.

Condo residents
Your home, your city

Stronger renter protections, transparent condo board rules, and a mayor who takes vertical communities seriously.

Non-voters
This one's different

If you've tuned out because no one ever runs for you — Mo is running for you. Real policy. Real presence. Real accountability.
