About Mo Khan · Campaign boilerplate

Mo Khan is the 2026 independent candidate for Mayor of Toronto. His platform focuses on housing acceleration, local economic growth, small business support, a managed night economy, AI modernization of City Hall, and youth and student retention. Mo is running on a commitment to make Toronto faster to build in, easier to live in, more open, and more supportive of the people who have been left behind by a city that moves too slowly. For more information, visit mofortoronto.ca or contact press@mofortoronto.ca.
