This city
needs you

Campaigns are won by people, not ads. Whether you have one hour or one hundred, there's a place for you in this movement. Pick your role and let's get to work.

How you can help

Pick your role

Every action counts. The campaign is built on people doing what they can, where they are, with what they have.

🚪
Knock doors

The single most powerful thing you can do. Join a canvass team in your ward and talk to real voters about real issues.

📞
Phone bank

Call from anywhere — your couch, your office, your kitchen. We'll give you the script, the list, and the training.

🏠
Host a house party

Invite your neighbours, we'll send a speaker and materials. Your home, your community, your impact.

📣
Share on social

Post, share, repost, remix. Every person you reach is a potential voter. Tag us @mofortoronto.

🎓
Campus organizing

Students for Mo — organize at your university, college, or school. We'll support you with materials, speakers, and training.

💰
Fundraise

Host a fundraiser, or just share the donate page with your network. Every dollar funds organizing in your ward.
